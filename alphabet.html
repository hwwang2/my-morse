How to translate content not in the alphata?

Morse code encryption only supports characters, numbers, and punctuation marks, and is case-insensitive. This tool extends it to support the encoding and decoding of complex characters (Principle: first convert the characters to Unicode encoding and then convert them to Morse code)..
